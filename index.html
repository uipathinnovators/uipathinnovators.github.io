The UiPath RPA Innovators Club at KLS Gogte Institute of Technology is a hub for students passionate about Robotic Process Automation (RPA).

Established in August 2024, our mission is to foster innovation through UiPath's cutting-edge automation tools. As part of the UiPath Academic Alliance since 2020, we provide our members with exclusive access to industry-leading resources and certifications.

Our club focuses on enhancing technical skills and solving real-world automation challenges. We host workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects, empowering students to stay at the forefront of digital transformation.

Join us to explore, innovate, and shape the future of automation!
